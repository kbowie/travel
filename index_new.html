Route Overview

We’ll begin our journey in a square haunted by the restless souls of beautiful young women executed for witchcraft. Discover who watches over Lisbon and who gifted Brazil its independence.

José will guide you to the finest examples of an architectural style born in Portugal during the Age of Discovery—magnificent facades adorned with stone lacework that will transport you centuries back in time.

Our ghostly guide will unveil the secret of a popular Portuguese dessert, still guarded by monks away from prying eyes. He’ll show you a remarkable spot that perfectly illustrates the influence of the Arab world on local culture.

There’s so much to say about Lisbon and Portugal as a whole, but José has handpicked the most intriguing and fascinating moments to share with you during this two-hour tour.

What makes this tour special?

This isn't just a regular audio guide - it's an immersive theatrical experience featuring professional voice actors, carefully crafted sound effects, and an engaging storyline. The Ghost King, your spectral guide, brings history to life while mixing in humor, spooky moments, and insider knowledge about modern Portuguese life.

How does the tour work?

Simply arrive at the starting point, open the app, put on your headphones, and step into a different dimension. You'll have access to a real-time interactive map, but don't worry - the Ghost King will guide you through every turn and stop. The audio is perfectly synchronized with the route, creating a seamless experience.

How flexible is the tour?

Entirely at your pace! You can pause anytime for photos, refreshments, or rest; split the tour across multiple days; take detours as you wish; and stop for recommended wine tastings and local dishes. The tour is designed to help you reach Lisbon's highest points while avoiding steep climbs, making it comfortable even in this city of seven hills.

What kind of content can I expect?

The tour combines historical storytelling through an engaging audio performance, lesser-known facts about famous landmarks, hidden gems off the typical tourist path, local cultural insights and contemporary Portuguese life, culinary and wine recommendations, and occasional jokes and spooky moments from your ghostly guide.

How immersive is the experience?

Thanks to professional voice acting, masterful sound design, and a dynamic script, you'll feel like you're walking through living history. The streets of Lisbon become your theater, where past meets present in an engaging performance.

Who is this tour suitable for?

This tour is recommended for visitors aged 16+ due to historical content, wine tasting references, and some mildly spooky elements. It's perfect for history enthusiasts who prefer entertainment over dry facts, culture lovers seeking authentic local insights, photography enthusiasts looking for unique perspectives, food and wine aficionados interested in local recommendations, and anyone wanting to explore Lisbon beyond the typical tourist experience.
